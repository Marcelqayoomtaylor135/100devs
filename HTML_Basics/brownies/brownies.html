Deep Dish Brownies

Prep Time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  1. Butter
  2. White sugar
  3. Vanilla extract
  4. 3 eggs
  5. Cocoa powder

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 175 degrees C and Grease an 8 inch pan
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter, sugar and vanilla
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time
  4. Mix dry ingredients then gradually blend into egg mixture
  5. Pour ingredients into pan and bake for 40 to 45 minutes

Tip

Aliminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly, and make clean-up easier
